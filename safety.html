Our Commitment

Our Safety Philosophy

Queensland Amusement Hire maintains the highest possible standards of safety across all operations. Our comprehensive safety management system encompasses daily pre-operation inspections, scheduled preventive maintenance, and regular independent safety audits.

We work closely with Workplace Health & Safety Queensland and comply with all applicable Australian Standards for amusement devices. Our operators are trained to the highest standards, and we hold $20 million in public liability insurance for your complete peace of mind.
