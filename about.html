Our Story

Brisbane's Largest Amusement Hire Company

As Brisbane's largest amusement hire company, we've been servicing festivals and events all over Australia for over 60 years. Our expertise and professionalism has been developed through work at major Australian events including the Brisbane EKKA, Royal Easter Show, Paniyiri Festival, Gold Coast Show, and many more.

Our well-trained, presentable, and friendly staff create safe environments where families can make lasting memories. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of safety and customer service in the amusement industry.
