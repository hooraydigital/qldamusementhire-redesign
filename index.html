About Us

Brisbane's Largest Amusement Hire Company

As Brisbane's largest amusement hire company, we've been servicing festivals and events all over Australia for over 60 years. Our expertise and professionalism has been developed through work at major Australian events.

Our well-trained, presentable, and friendly staff create safe environments where families can make lasting memories. From the biggest music festivals to intimate school fetes, we bring the same level of dedication and fun to every event.
